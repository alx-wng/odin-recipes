Steak

Description

Debbie's special recipe

Ingredients

  • Steak
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Steak seasoning

Preparation

  1. Fully defrost the steak.
  2. Season both sides of the steak with salt, pepper, and steak seasoning.
  3. Allow steak to sit for 30 minutes.
  4. Heat pan with oil until oil is shimmering.
  5. Cook each side of the steak for 3-5 minutes, depending on personal preference.
  6. Let steak rest for 5 minutes before eating.
