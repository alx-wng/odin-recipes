Instant Ramen

Description

The college student special: instant ramen

Ingredients

  • 1 bag of instant ramen
  • 1 egg
  • water

Preparation

  1. Boil enough water to cover the noodles. Adjust the amount of water to your preference.
  2. Open the bag of instant ramen and add the soup powder to the water. Add the noodles to the soup and turn down the heat.
  3. Cook noodles according to package instructions. Move noodles from pot into bowl and add egg into pot. Cook egg to your prefered doneness.
  4. Pour soup and egg into bowl with ramen. Enjoy!
